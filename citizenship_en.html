REGULATION ON OBTAINING CITIZENSHIP OF THE KINGDOM OF NESORIA ⸻ REGULATION on the Procedure for Obtaining Citizenship of the Kingdom of Nesoria ⸻ I. General Provisions 1. This Regulation defines the procedure and conditions for obtaining citizenship of the Kingdom of Nesoria. 2. In accordance with Article 6 of the Constitution of the Kingdom of Nesoria, citizenship is granted on the basis of voluntary acceptance of the ethical contract and completion of a selection procedure. 3. Citizenship of Nesoria is an ethical and voluntary status based on personal responsibility, trust and respect for human dignity. 4. Citizenship is not an automatic right and is granted only as a result of individual consideration. ⸻ II. Preliminary Requirements To begin the procedure, the applicant must: 1. Independently familiarize themselves with: • the Constitution of the Kingdom of Nesoria; • the Manifesto of the Kingdom of Nesoria; • the core values and principles of the State. 2. Consciously accept and share the following fundamental principles: • recognition of human dignity as the highest value; • respect for the sovereignty and borders of other states; • rejection of discrimination based on origin, language or place of birth; • priority of the human being over citizenship. ⸻ III. Initial Application 1. To begin the procedure, the applicant submits an official application to the email address of the Kingdom of Nesoria. 2. The application must include: • country of current residence; • brief personal information; • motivation for seeking citizenship of Nesoria; • confirmation of having read the Constitution. 3. The initial application is reviewed by the King of Nesoria. ⸻ IV. Ethical Test 1. If the initial application is approved, the applicant is sent an ethical test. 2. The test aims to assess: • worldview of the applicant; • understanding of Nesorian values; • respect for human rights; • capacity for responsible thinking. 3. The test has no formal scoring system and is evaluated individually. ⸻ V. Interview 1. If the test is successfully passed, the applicant receives an invitation to an interview. 2. The interview is conducted online. 3. The purpose of the interview is: • personal acquaintance; • evaluation of sincerity; • confirmation of alignment with state values. ⸻ VI. Decision 1. The final decision on granting citizenship is made by the King of Nesoria. 2. The decision is based on: • the initial application; • the test results; • the interview outcome. 3. In case of a positive decision, citizenship and a Nesorian passport are granted. 4. In case of refusal, reasons may not be disclosed. ⸻ VII. Legal Consequences 1. From the moment of obtaining citizenship, the citizen receives full trust from the State (Article 7 of the Constitution). 2. The citizen undertakes to: • comply with the Constitution of Nesoria; • respect the borders and laws of other states; • refrain from actions against humanity. 3. In accordance with Article 8 of the Constitution, deprivation of citizenship is final and irreversible. ⸻ VIII. Final Provision Citizenship of the Kingdom of Nesoria is not a formal status but an ethical contract between the individual and the State, based on mutual trust, responsibility and respect for human dignity. ⸻ Signed: King of Nesoria Valery Tovbin January 20, 2026
