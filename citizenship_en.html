Citizenship Act of the Kingdom of Nesoria

Preamble

This Act defines the principles, procedure and conditions for obtaining citizenship of the Kingdom of Nesoria in accordance with the Constitution and ethical values of the State.

Article 1. General Principle
Citizenship of Nesoria is a form of ethical trust and is not based on origin, nationality, language or place of birth.
Article 2. Voluntary Basis
Citizenship may be obtained solely on a voluntary basis at the initiative of the applicant.
Article 3. Preliminary ознакомление
Before applying, the candidate must familiarize themselves with the Constitution, manifestos and core principles of the Kingdom of Nesoria.
Article 4. Application
The candidate submits an official letter to the state contact address indicating the country of residence and motivation.
Article 5. Testing
The candidate receives a test designed to assess understanding of the values, ethics and principles of the State.
Article 6. Interview
If the test is successfully passed, the candidate is invited to an online interview with representatives of the State.
Article 7. Decision
The final decision on granting citizenship is made by the Monarch and the relevant authorities of the Kingdom of Nesoria.
Article 8. Trust
From the moment of obtaining citizenship, the State grants the citizen full ethical and institutional trust.
Article 9. Revocation
In case of violation of the Constitution, ethical principles and disrespect for the sovereignty of other states, citizenship is revoked permanently and cannot be restored.
