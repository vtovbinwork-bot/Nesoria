DECLARATION OF ESTABLISHMENT OF THE KINGDOM OF NESORIA

Preamble

We, the people and founders of the Kingdom of Nesoria, recognizing the supreme value of human dignity, freedom, and responsibility, hereby proclaim the establishment of the Kingdom of Nesoria as a virtual ethical state.

Principles of the Kingdom:
1. Human dignity and personal freedom are the highest values.
2. Freedom of movement is regarded as a form of trust, not an automatic right.
3. The Kingdom respects the sovereignty and borders of all countries and does not interfere in their internal affairs.
4. The state has no territory or army, existing in a virtual form.
5. The source of power is the people, exercised through the parliament and the monarch.
6. Citizenship is granted only after voluntary acceptance of the ethical contract and completion of the selection procedure.
Formalization of this Declaration:
- establishment of the Kingdom of Nesoria;
- adoption of the Constitution, Public and Internal Manifestos, Crown Act, External Declaration, and Citizenship Act;
- recognition by the Founder and governing bodies of these documents as the supreme guidance of the Kingdom.
Signatures:
King of Nesoria / Founder: Valery Tovbin
Date: January 22, 2026
