Constitution of the Kingdom of Nesoria

Adopted and signed by the King of Nesoria, Valery Tovbin
January 20, 2026

Preamble

The Kingdom of Nesoria is established as an ethical virtual state, based on the recognition of human dignity, personal freedom and the equality of all people regardless of origin, language, citizenship or place of birth.

The State recognizes the human being before their citizenship and affirms that no person may be restricted solely due to the place of their birth.

Section I. Foundations of the State System

Article 1. The Kingdom of Nesoria is a constitutional parliamentary monarchy of an ethical type.
Article 2. Nesoria is a virtual sovereign state and does not claim territorial possessions.
Article 3. The source of power in Nesoria is the people represented by the citizens and the King.
Article 4. The State has no armed forces and does not participate in military conflicts.

Section II. Citizenship

Article 5. Citizenship of Nesoria is ethical and voluntary.
Article 6. Citizenship may be obtained only after passing a selection procedure and confirming compliance with the values of the State.
Article 7. A citizen holding a passport of Nesoria enjoys full trust from the State.
Article 8. In case of deprivation of citizenship, re-acquisition of citizenship is impossible.

Section III. Rights and Duties

Article 9. The State guarantees the protection of human dignity of every citizen.
Article 10. Citizens are obliged to respect the sovereignty, laws and borders of other states.
Article 11. Violation of the principles of humanity and international respect constitutes grounds for deprivation of citizenship.

Section IV. Monarchy

Article 12. The Head of State is the King of Nesoria.
Article 13. The King exercises his powers within the framework of the Constitution.
Article 14. The monarchy is hereditary and limited.

Final Provisions

Article 15. The Constitution is the supreme legal act of the Kingdom of Nesoria.
Article 16. All state acts must comply with this Constitution.
Signed:
King of Nesoria Valery Tovbin
January 20, 2026
