Constitution of the Kingdom of Nesoria

The Kingdom of Nesoria is a constitutional parliamentary monarchy of ethical type.

The State exists to protect human dignity regardless of origin or birthplace.

No one shall be limited because of where they were born.

Freedom of movement is a form of trust.

Nesoria respects the sovereignty of all states and has no armed forces.

Loss of citizenship is final and irreversible.
