Constitution of the Kingdom of Nesoria

Preamble

We, the people of the Kingdom of Nesoria, recognizing the value of the human person, acknowledging human dignity as the highest value, affirming freedom, trust and responsibility as the foundation of the state system, adopt this Constitution.

Article 1. Form of State
The Kingdom of Nesoria is a constitutional parliamentary monarchy of an ethical type.
Article 2. Purpose of the State
The State exists to protect human dignity regardless of origin, language and place of birth and recognizes the human being before their citizenship.
Article 3. Ideology
A person cannot be restricted solely because they were born elsewhere. Freedom of movement is regarded as a form of trust, not an automatic right.
Article 4. Sovereignty
The Kingdom of Nesoria respects the sovereignty and borders of all states and does not interfere in the internal affairs of other countries.
Article 5. Nature of the State
Nesoria is a virtual ethical state and does not claim territorial sovereignty.
Article 6. Citizenship
Citizenship of Nesoria is granted on the basis of voluntary acceptance of the ethical contract and completion of the selection procedure.
Article 7. Trust
From the moment of obtaining citizenship, the citizen receives full trust from the State.
Article 8. Deprivation of Citizenship
Deprivation of citizenship is allowed exclusively in cases of serious violation of ethical principles and respect for state borders. Deprivation of citizenship is final and irreversible.
Article 9. Absence of an Army
The Kingdom of Nesoria has no armed forces and does not participate in military conflicts.
Article 10. Source of Power
The source of power is the people, who exercise it through the parliament and the monarch.
Article 11. The Monarch
The Monarch is the Head of State and the guarantor of the Constitution. The power of the Monarch is limited by the Constitution and the parliament.
Article 12. Languages
The official state languages are Russian and English.
Signed:
King of Nesoria Valery Tovbin
January 20, 2026
